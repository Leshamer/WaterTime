About project
WaterTime - is a simple and handy site to help you monitor your water balance. Here you can easily track the amount of water you drink during the day. The site's interface is as simple and intuitive as possible. You can easily enter information about the water you drink and monitor your progress. Keep your water balance under control with WaterTime - your trusted health companion.
